Release Satellite

The launch is crucial to safely bring our highly sensitive instruments beyond the disruptive influence of the Earth's atmosphere, where unobstructed X-ray observations are possible.

Inside the nose cone is the NEBULA XPLORER, technologically armored against the extreme G-forces and acoustic vibrations of the flight.

Once in space, the launch vehicle positions the satellite into a stable orbit, an absolute prerequisite for uninterrupted and precise scientific measurements.
